15) AD value? 

Definition
Administrative Distance (AD) is a numerical value used by routers to measure trustworthiness of a routing information source.
Purpose

When multiple routes to same destination exist:

router chooses lowest AD

Lowest AD = most trusted route.

Important Notes
AD is local to router
not advertised to other routers
resolves routing conflicts
Default AD Values
Route Source	AD
Connected Route	0
Static Route	1
EIGRP (Internal)	90
OSPF	110
RIP	120
Unknown/Untrusted	255
